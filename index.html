Min Woo Sun

I am a PhD student in the Biomedical Informatics program at Stanford University, advised by Rob Tibshirani. I work on statistical and machine learning methods to find solutions to biomedical problems. Recently, I have been interested in high-dimensional multiomic datasets and using graph based models to uncover insights. Prior to joining Stanford, I graduated from University of California--Los Angeles UCLA with a B.S. in Statistics and B.A. in Economics. Afterwards, I worked at Invitae as a Bioinformatics Scientist taking data-driven approaches to improving the germline DNA sequencing pipeline. You can download my most recent CV here.
